# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone.
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept random payment requests.
- Always verify the sender before clicking on any links or attachments.
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
- Regularly update your apps and devices to fix security issues.
- Check app permissions and install apps only from trusted sources.
- Be cautious of calls pretending to be from banks or customer support.
- Log out from shared or public devices after use.
