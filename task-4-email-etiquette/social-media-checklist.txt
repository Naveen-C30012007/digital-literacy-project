# Responsible Social Media Checklist for College Students

## Do’s
- Be respectful and polite in posts and comments.
- Think before posting anything online.
- Keep your account privacy settings secure.
- Share only verified and accurate information.
- Use social media for learning and networking.

## Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information.
- Don’t post offensive or harmful content.
- Don’t spread fake news or rumors.
- Don’t engage in online fights or bullying.
- Don’t waste too much time scrolling.
